The App is designed to respect your privacy, and as such, we do not collect any personal data from you. We do not access or store any information about your usage patterns, device information, or any other identifiable information.

The App automatically syncs your data to your personal iCloud account. This process is entirely handled by Apple's iCloud infrastructure, and we do not have access to or store any of your files.

Unless stated otherwise, this Application processes payments through external payment service providers, such as credit card companies or banks. Users are typically required to provide payment details and personal information directly to these providers. The Application is not involved in collecting or processing this information; it only receives notifications from the payment service provider regarding successful payment completion.
